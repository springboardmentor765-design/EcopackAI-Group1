About EcoPackAI

AI-Powered Sustainable Packaging Recommendation System

The Mission

EcoPackAI is an intelligent decision-support platform designed to help industries select sustainable packaging materials without compromising product safety or cost efficiency. The system leverages machine learning models to predict packaging cost and carbon footprint, enabling data-driven sustainability decisions.

Problem Addressed

  • Overdependence on non-biodegradable packaging materials
  • Increasing carbon emissions from packaging processes
  • Lack of intelligent tools for sustainable material selection
  • Difficulty in balancing cost, durability, and environmental impact

Technology Stack

  • Programming: Python (Flask)
  • Machine Learning: Random Forest, XGBoost
  • Database: PostgreSQL
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Chart.js
  • Reporting: ReportLab (PDF), OpenPyXL (Excel)
